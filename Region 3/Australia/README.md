# Australia (VK)

Amateur radio in Australia has a long history. There are clubs in every state
and territory and there is an active community of radio amateurs with regular
activities and events.

The regulator in Australia is the Australian Communications and Media Authority
([ACMA](https://www.acma.gov.au/)). It controls license approvals and callsign
allocations.

The hobby is represented to Government by individual submissions and two bodies,
the Wireless Institute of Australia ([WIA](https://wia.org.au/)) and the Radio
Amateur Society of Australia ([RASA](https://vkradioamateurs.org/)).

Clubs are often affiliated with one or other of the representative bodies and
share resources such as insurance and events.

As of 2024-02-19, Australia moved to a class licensing system, where "a
qualified person with a suitable call sign may operate an amateur station" .. in
other words, you need to hold a qualification and a callsign in order to
transmit on amateur radio frequencies.


# Australian Communications and Media Authority (ACMA) information

The regulator controls the radio spectrum on behalf of the Government and in
doing so also manages license allocation and certification. Radio amateurs are
expected to be qualified and hold a current amateur callsign.

* [Amateur Radio homepage](https://www.acma.gov.au/amateur-radio)
* [Qualifications to operate amateur radio](https://www.acma.gov.au/qualifications-operate-amateur-radio)
* [Callsign Register](https://www.acma.gov.au/are-you-looking-amateur-call-sign)

* [Overseas amateurs visiting Australia](https://www.acma.gov.au/overseas-amateurs-visiting-australia)
* [Amateur radio resources](https://www.acma.gov.au/amateur-radio-resources)


# Wireless Institute of Australia (WIA) information

The Wireless Institute of Australia ([WIA](https://wia.org.au/)) is the oldest
amateur radio association in the world and a representative to the regulator.

* [Getting Licensed](https://www.wia.org.au/licenses/licensing/about/)
* [Foundation License](https://www.wia.org.au/licenses/foundation/about/)
* [Standard License](https://www.wia.org.au/licenses/standard/about/)
* [Advanced License](https://www.wia.org.au/licenses/advanced/about/)


# Radio Amateur Society of Australia (RASA) information

The Radio Amateur Society of Australia ([RASA](https://vkradioamateurs.org/))
represents and promotes the hobby of Amateur Radio in Australia.

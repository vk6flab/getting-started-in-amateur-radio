# Playground

Amateur radio is a vast playground using any excuse to get on air and have fun.
With a massive global community, the hobby has activities all day, every day.
This list gives you somewhere to start on your journey.


* Online receivers:

  - [WebSDR](http://websdr.org) - a directory of online receivers that you can
    listen to all over the world.

  - [KiwiSDR](http://rx.kiwisdr.com) - a directory of online receivers that you
    can listen to all over the world.

  - [RTL-SDR](https://www.rtl-sdr.com) - projects and information about and
    related to the "RTL-SDR" dongle, a cheap USB device that can be used to receive
    radio frequencies.


* Repeaters

  - [RepeaterBook](https://www.repeaterbook.com/) - global directory of repeaters


* Social media:

  - [mastodon.radio](https://mastodon.radio) - fediverse instance with a focus
    on radio amateurs.

  - [lemmy.radio](https://lemmy.radio) - fediverse instance with a focus on
    radio amateurs.


* On-Air Nets:

  - [F-troop](https://ftroop.groups.io)


* Activities:

  - [Activations on Air](https://github.com/vk6flab/activations-on-the-air)

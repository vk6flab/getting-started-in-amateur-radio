# Playground

Amateur radio is a vast playground using any excuse to get on air and have fun. With a massive global community, the hobby has activities all day, every day. This list gives you somewhere to start on your journey.

* Online receivers:
  - [WebSDR](http://websdr.org)
  - [KiwiSDR](http://rx.kiwisdr.com)
  - [RTL-SDR](https://www.rtlsdr.com)

* Social media:
  - [Mastodon](https://mastodon.radio)
  - [Lemmy](https://lemmy.radio)

* On-Air Nets:
  - [F-troop](https://ftroop.groups.io)

* Activities:
  - [Activations on Air](https://github.com/vk6flab/activations-on-the-air)

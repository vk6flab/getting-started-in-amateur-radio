# Getting Started in Amateur Radio

Getting Started in the hobby of Amateur Radio can be a challenge if you don't
know anyone in the community. This collection of documents and resources aims
to help you find your way.

This repository is intended to serve as a landing spot in response to the
question: "Where do I start in Amateur Radio?"

* [Start Here](README.md)


## Structure

There is a generic component to every amateur radio journey, mainly to do
with how the hobby and the community surrounding it is organised and
structured. This information should be captured in the main landing page.

In addition there are activities that can be undertaken without an amateur
license, they should be captured in the [Playground](playground.md) page,
noting the comments about **content** below.

For each country, there are specific requirements and considerations that
are likely to change from time-to-time, mostly enforced by the various
regulatory bodies and officials controlling radio spectrum. Each country
is represented by a folder with resources pertinent to that particular
entity.

You'll note that the world is split into three regions. While this might look
arbitrary, it actually reflects the structure adopted by the International
Telecommunications Union (ITU) and the International Amateur Radio Union (IARU).


## Content

The intent is for this project to have links to the actual information rather
than host copies of that information, so instead of adding a specific Syllabus,
add a link to a web page that has the information for the Syllabus and the link
to it. See the [Australia](./Region%203/Australia/) page for examples.

The reasoning for this is two fold:

* From a legal perspective, many documents are copyrighted and not permitted to
  be hosted elsewhere without permission and are subject to takedown notices and
  the associated legal effort to comply.

* From a maintenance perspective, an update to a document is likely to go
  unnoticed by anyone other than the author, which means that any document hosted
  here will be out of date for an undetermined time, not to mention the effort
  required to upload new versions.


## Updates

This is likely to be updated from time-to-time. Changes and patches are
welcome and expected. If you do make a change, please include a date for
when a particular change occurred, so future visitors have a chance to
determine if the information is still current or not.
